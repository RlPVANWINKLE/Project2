Software Development

What are we?

Here at Ogden weber technical college we are about you. We allow you to complete Your programs with no maximum speeds. Move as fast as you can. We are about getting your life started with minimal effort once graduated we help you get a job in the related field.

What is Software Development?

Software development is the creation of software and programs that are on computers, phones, and websites. Our courses are here to teach you what is happening as well as how to write the code for these ideas and concepts.

Is Software Development for you?

Everyone can write code. However not everyone can develope and understand the code. Problem solving is our #1 thing to get down. We need to look at errors as a challenge rather than a problem. If you can problem solve and understand that everything we do is a challenge and meant to make you think!
